- Existence of a hazardous condition: an inadequately maintained machine that can fail unexpectedly is a hazard.
- Recognition of the hazard: the records showing notifications to maintenance and supervisors establish that the hazard was known or should have been known to the employer (a key element for a General Duty Clause violation).

- D (NIOSH guidelines): Informative and useful as best-practice guidance but not an enforceable legal duty.
